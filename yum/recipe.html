Lemon Pie

In a small saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup water. Bring to boil
and stir to dissolve sugar. Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate until chilled.

Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp. In pitcher, stir
together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 cups water.

1 cup sugar
2 cups lemonade
8 cups water
